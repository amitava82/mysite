A few words about Me

Hi, I'm full-stack javascript developer with over 8 years professional experience building full stack web applications. My strength is in Node.Js backend and React,Redux ecosystem for frontend.

Before starting my freelancing journey, I worked with various startups in India and USA. I've worked as individual contributor, team member and played team lead role as well.

As freelancer, I have experience working as individual contributor as well as in team member. I've worked on short few days projects to projects that went over a year, working from an idea to a successful launch. I collaborate well with distributed remote team.

Most days, I work on building RESTful APIs using Express, AWS Lambda or Firebase functions, documenting using Swagger/Open API, building structured React components and user interfaces of various complexities. Other times, I bring life to PSD or Invisio design with HTML, CSS & Javascript magic.
